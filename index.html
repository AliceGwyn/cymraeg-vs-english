Cymraeg vs English

An exploration of Cymraeg(Welsh) vs English
in Cymru(Wales) from 2001-2021

A Brief History

Cymru and England have always had a contentious relationship, and Cymraeg, Welsh, has historically and systemically been oppressed. More recently, efforts have been introduced to restore Cymraeg as a major language in Cymru, including an increased focus on early education in Cymraeg and more government access available in Cymraeg as well as English.
